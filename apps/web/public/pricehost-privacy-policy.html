Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PriceGhost AI, developed by Nexa Team. Your privacy is paramount to us. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our browser extension.

2. Data Collection & Usage

PriceGhost AI is designed with a Privacy-First philosophy:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • No Accounts: You do not need to create an account to use the extension.
  • Local Storage: Your product wishlist and settings are stored locally on your device via chrome.storage.local. We do not have access to this data on our servers.
  • Search Queries: When the extension scans for prices, it only sends the product title to the search engines of supported retailers (Amazon, Walmart, eBay). This is done anonymously.

3. Third-Party Websites

Our extension interacts with third-party websites (Amazon.com, Walmart.com, eBay.com). Please note that these websites have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them as we have no control over their practices.

4. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) for all data fetching. Because we do not store your data on our servers, the risk of data breaches on our end is non-existent.

5. Compliance

PriceGhost AI complies with the Vietnam Law on Cybersecurity and international privacy standards. We do not engage in unauthorized data scraping or malicious activities.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our official homepage: nexa-tech-team.vercel.app.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our website.
